Chinese New Year is on this Friday. So here I will introduce some fun facts about the CNY.


      There are 12 Chinese zodiacs. This year is a dog year.

      Fish is a metaphor in Chinese New Year, means "the left". The deeper meaning is that wish you had some left (of money) at the end of the year.

      Coins and firecrackers are two essential items in CNY. There is also another symbol to represent the coins in China:. Parents and the elders will give the kids some coins, and they will light the firecrackers together!
